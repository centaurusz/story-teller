Welcome Adventurer!

StortyTeller is grimoire of TTRPG campaigns. "But what's a TTRPG?" you might ask, well here's a whole paragraph we took from wikipedia:

A tabletop role-playing game (typically abbreviated as TRPG or TTRPG), also known as a pwn-and-paper role-playing game, is a form of role-playing game (RPG) in which the participants describe their character's based on their characterization, and the actions succeed or fail according to a set formal system of rules and guidelines. Within the rules, players have the freedom to improvise; their choices shape the direction and outcome of the game.

The terms pen-and-paper and tabletop are generally only used to distinguish this format of RPG from other formats, since neither pen and paper nor a table are stricly necessary.
